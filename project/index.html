About Us

The early tale of Burger House brims with nostalgia and comfort. It all started from making homemade burgers for our neighbours and their children. Thus, we find ourselves in a small shop, wanting to flip patties and make burgers for the entire neighbourhood. Burger House is a place where you can seek comfort food and comfort within the neighbourhood.
